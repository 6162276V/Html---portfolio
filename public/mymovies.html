My top 3 movies of all time

The lion king

A classic movie that is timeless for the ages regarding the passing of the crown from mufasa to simba.

Zootopia

A theme of animals here, regarding Judy Hopps' case to determine why the predators in zootopia have gone savage.

Avatar the last airbender

Not really a movie, but a series nonetheless that tells the timeless tale of the avatar, and Aang's journey to master the four elements.
